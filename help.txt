Welcome to the ApplianceApp !!
This app have 7 commands option:
Add, List, Modify, Delete, Find, Help, Quit
1. Add: enter your appliance name, brand and room
2. List: display all the appliances
3. Modify: update your appliances detail. You can choose the appliances by name
4. Delete: remove the appliance out of the your list.
5. Find: find the appliance by enter its name. An message will indicate whether the appliance is exist in the list or not.
6. Help: assist you to use the app.
7. Quit: exit the program.
